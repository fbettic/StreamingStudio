Available Platforms

The following platforms are available to be linked to your Streaming Studio account. If you already have an account on one of these platforms, you can link it by clicking the "Login" button. If you do not have an account on the desired platform and wish to create one, click "Sign up". Once the account is created, it will automatically be linked to your Streaming Studio account. You can unlink a linked account at any time by clicking the "Unlink" button, which will appear after the account is linked.
